Design Process

These illustrations are inspired by imagery found in a story about Buddhist teachings, titled 'Roaring Bulls With No Fight'. Both illustrations highlight the unthinking consumption of one's surrounding. They chronicle living on a damaged land and being in a perpetuating, vicious cycle of harm. Through my works, I derive a form of self-expression and an opportunity to vocalise my hopes for a sustainable, equitable and inclusive world.
